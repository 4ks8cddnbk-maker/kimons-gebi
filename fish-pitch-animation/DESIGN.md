## Style Prompt

.fish is glossy Y2K social software: aqua chrome, MySpace window chrome, rounded pills, glass panels, tiny OS window dots, iPod-radio controls, soft grid backgrounds, and playful but polished product UI. Keep the animation clean enough for a pitch, with cinematic camera moves over actual app-like screens.

## Colors

- `#19c6ff` primary aqua glow
- `#2f82bd` deep chrome blue
- `#f8fbff` glass paper
- `#ff6e9f` social pink accent
- `#28c840` online green
- `#ffcf4a` event gold
- `#172235` ink

## Typography

- Use `"Lucida Grande", "Helvetica Neue", Arial, sans-serif` for product UI.
- Use `"Arial Black", "Bungee", sans-serif` for short punchy pitch words.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not show the preview or waiting page.
- Do not switch to a dark tech style.
- Do not use generic SaaS dashboards.
- Do not flatten the glossy .fish/MySpace/iPod aesthetic.
- Do not use German on-screen copy; this pitch animation is English.
